About Us

Our Services

Cloud Sphere is able to offer a wide range of services using either internal skills or vast network of contacts aquired through more than 25 years or professional networking.

Whether your requirement is for cloud based services or on premise we will be able to provide you with a solution for all of your requirements.

Our Approach

Cloud Sphere are passionate about technology but more so about providing our clients with the best possible service. We do not shoehorn our customer into a solution but rather work to find the solution that best fits their requirements. We will work tirelessly with you to make sure we provide exactly what you want and make sure what you want is what you need.

​

We are vendor, platform and technology agnostic and this gives us the ability to provide the best solution to meet your exact requirements regardless of where you are currently in your technology lifecycle.

Why Us?

The staff at Cloud Sphere have worked in many sectors across a multitude of household names supplying the very best in consulting services.

We have worked on some very large projects and have always delivered on time and within budget.

Our customers come first and we always deliver what we promise.
